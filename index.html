About

Who Is New Voodoo

New Voodoo is currently the recording, performance, mixing, and production work of Dorian Duerinckx. The project is built around dark atmosphere, guitar-driven weight, and a studio-first approach that carries one vision from idea to finished track without sanding off its edge.

Dorian Duerinckx currently records and performs, as well as handles studio mixing and production, across the project’s material. The result is layered, cinematic, and intentionally restless rather than polished into something safe.

  • Electric Guitar
  • Acoustic Guitar
  • Bass Guitar (Special guest Thad Bonduris)
  • Keyboards
  • Drum Programming
  • Vocals (Special guest Gemini)
